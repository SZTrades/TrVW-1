📊 **ATR Momentum Oscillator - Multi-Asset 4H**

🎯 **Purpose**: Identify momentum exhaustion and potential reversal points using normalized ATR movements with Bollinger Band confluence.

🔍 **How It Works**:
- Calculates biggest price move over X periods
- Normalizes by dividing by current ATR 
- Shows when momentum reaches extreme levels (±3.0+ ATR units)
- Adds Bollinger Band filter for confluence signals

⚡ **Signal Logic**:
🔴 **Red Dot (Sell Signal)**: Move/ATR ≥ +3.0 + Price > Upper BB = Bullish exhaustion
🟢 **Green Dot (Buy Signal)**: Move/ATR ≤ -3.0 + Price < Lower BB = Bearish exhaustion

📈 **Asset Presets**:
🥇 **Gold**: 12 periods, ±3.0 threshold, ATR(14) - Standard trending moves
🥈 **Silver**: 8 periods, ±2.5 threshold, ATR(10) - More volatile, faster signals  
💵 **DXY**: 18 periods, ±4.0 threshold, ATR(20) - Slower moves, higher threshold
⚙️ **Custom**: Full manual control for any asset

🎛️ **Settings Groups**:
- **Asset Presets**: Quick setup for Gold/Silver/DXY/Custom
- **Core Settings**: Lookback periods (3-48), ATR length (5-50)
- **Thresholds**: Upper/Lower momentum levels (±1.0 to ±8.0)
- **Bollinger Bands**: BB length/multiplier for confluence

📊 **Table Info**:
- **ATR**: Current ATR value
- **Move/ATR**: Current momentum ratio (green=bullish, red=bearish)
- **Status**: NORMAL / EXTREME ↑ / EXTREME ↓

🧠 **Best Use Cases**:
- 4H timeframe for swing reversals
- Confluence with other momentum indicators
- Counter-trend entries at extreme levels
- Not for trending breakout trades

💡 **Pro Tip**: Best signals occur when oscillator hits extreme AND price is outside Bollinger Bands. Single conditions may give false signals.

Perfect for traders who want to catch swing reversals with data-driven momentum exhaustion signals.
